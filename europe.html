Introduction

Europe, cradle of the vampire myth (from Transylvania to Slavic legends), hosts a dynamic yet fragmented subculture today. Influenced by gothic literature and role-playing games like *Vampire: The Masquerade*, it counts thousands of members through meetups, LARP (Live Action Role-Playing), and forums. Note: While mostly peaceful, some isolating groups can drift toward emotional manipulation—contact local authorities if concerned.

Quick History

  • Origins: Slavic folklore (e.g., Romanian strigoi) and Victorian gothic. 1990s: Boom with Anne Rice and goth-punk.
  • Today: ~5,000-10,000 adherents estimated, via Discord/Reddit. Focus on "otherkin" identity (feeling non-human).
