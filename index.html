Welcome to a Responsible Exploration Space

This site catalogs the global vampire subculture: its historical origins, legitimate communities worldwide, and risks of sectarian drifts. Based on reliable sources, it aims to inform enthusiasts, concerned families, and researchers without judgment.

Key Objectives: Catalog groups (Europe to Oceania), alert on manipulations (e.g., historical cases like the Vampire Clan), and provide multilingual resources.

Why This Site?

Between myth and reality, the vampire subculture includes thousands of peaceful adherents. But isolated cases (e.g., financial manipulations in "pay-cults") warrant vigilance.
