Bibliography: Books, Press Articles, and Sites

This curated list covers academic books, press, and sites for a global view (Europe/Americas/Asia/Africa/Oceania). Priority to 2020-2025 sources; includes warnings on drifts. Download as PDF via this page.

Key Books

  • Academic: *Vampires Today: The Truth about Modern Vampirism* by Joseph Laycock (EN, 2009/2023 ed.) – US study on real identities.
  • Historical: *Speaking with Vampires: Rumor and History in Colonial Africa* by Luise White (EN, 2000) – African folklore, modern ties.
  • Modern: *Vampire Culture* by Maria Mellins (EN, 2011) – London/European communities.
  • Multilingual: *Vampire Nation* (Serbia, EN/SR, 2019) – Balkan myths; *Vampiros en Latinoamérica* (ES, 2022) – South America.

Press Articles (2025 Focus)

  • NY Post: "Inside the secret society of 'real life vampires'" (EN, June 8, 2025) – Australia/USA, international balls.
  • CNN: "Inside the world of real-life vampires in New Orleans and Atlanta" (EN, 2022/2025 update) – Psi/sanguinarian communities.
  • News.com.au: "US sociologist exposes a world of real vampires" (EN, 2015/2025 ref.) – Disinterest in pop culture.
  • Fodors: "16 Real Places Where Vampires Might Actually Exist" (EN, 2024) – Global spots (NOLA, Transylvania).
