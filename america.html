Introduction

The Americas blend indigenous folklore (e.g., Mexican chupacabras) with imported gothic subculture. In the US, New Orleans is the "vampire capital"; in South America, ties to spiritism. Estimates: 20,000+ North American members.

History

  • North: 1970s, Anne Rice influence. 1890s vampire panic (e.g., Mercy Brown).
  • South: Vampires as "brujos" in Andean legends.
