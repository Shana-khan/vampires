Introduction

Asia transforms the Western vampire myth through local folklors: from Japanese yōkai to Malaysian pontianak, blending with K-drama goth. Less structured than US "courts" (~3,000-6,000 members), communities are boosted by social media and video games (e.g., *Bloodborne*). Vigilance: Some online groups may promote dangerous rituals under "spirituality"—check moderations.

History and Context

  • Folklore: Vampires as Chinese "jiangshi" (hopping zombies) or Filipino "aswang" (bloodthirsty shapeshifters).
  • Modern: 2010s+, J-pop/K-pop influence (e.g., idol groups with vampire aesthetics). Events via Discord and Weibo.
